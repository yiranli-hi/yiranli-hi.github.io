My latest ***Nature Mental Health*** article is finally out! 🎉

🚩 This is also the **final publication of my PhD**.

Using the Dutch population register data from over **8 million individuals**, we found that people with autism have higher risks of several cardiometabolic conditions, with indications of **earlier onset** compared with non-autistic individuals.

Our results highlight the need for monitoring and treatment of cardiometabolic conditions among individuals with autism.
