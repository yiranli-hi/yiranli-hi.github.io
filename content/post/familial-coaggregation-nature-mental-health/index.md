Exciting news! 🎉 

Our study on the familial co-aggregation of autism, ADHD, and cardiometabolic conditions has just been published in ***Nature Mental Health***!

👨‍👩‍👧‍👧 The findings suggest a **partly shared familial liability** underlying the co-occurrence of neurodevelopmental and cardiometabolic conditions.
