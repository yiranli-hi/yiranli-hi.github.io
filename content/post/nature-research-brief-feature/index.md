Honored to share that our recent paper has been featured in a ***Nature Research Brief***! 🎉

The editors selected our study for a feature, noting its importance in understanding autism and health comorbidities.

👏 It is incredibly rewarding to see our work described as:

> *"presents the largest nationwide dataset and potentially most robust longitudinal analyses of elevated risk for cardiometabolic conditions in autism".*

We hope it informs future research and supports the development of more tailored interventions.
